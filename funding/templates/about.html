About

A simple funding system forked from Wownero made with:

  • Python 3.5+
  • Flask microframework
  • Postgres 9.5+
  • Redis

We do not keep access logs or install tracking cookies. All static resources (javascript/stylesheets/images) are self-hosted.

Firo is grateful to the Wownero team for open-sourcing their funding system.
